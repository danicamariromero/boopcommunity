Boop Runway

Sep 26

Don't just walk, strut! This annual event is for our adorable Boops who love to dress-up and shine on the floor. This event is a fund-raising and proceeds will be put to the fund for the Boops in our shelter.

Open Shelter

Oct 22

Quarterly, Boop Community holds an open-shelter event for prospect adopters. Although the shelter is always open for visitors through appointments, this day is not a normal day for our Boops. They have prepared well for this day to showcase their best looks and wonderful performances. Make sure to bring some treats and your cameras... and prepare your homes for it is sure you will go home with one adorable plus one after the event!
